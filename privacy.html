Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

GlideRead ("the App") is developed by Psylch. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data Collection

GlideRead does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No account or login required
  • No network requests to external servers

Local Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • EPUB files you import into the library
  • Reading progress and bookmarks
  • Display preferences (font size, theme, reading mode)

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and is not accessible to us or any third party.

File Access

GlideRead accesses files only when you explicitly import EPUB documents through the system file picker. The App does not access files without your action.

Third-Party Services

GlideRead uses no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

GlideRead does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version at this URL with a revised effective date.
